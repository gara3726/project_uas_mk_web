About

Creative Code 4.0

Creative Code 4.0 is the flagship annual technology festival organized by HIMATESI — the Information Technology Student Association. Under the theme Society 5.0: Technology for Humanity, this edition explores how technology can actively serve people, communities, and the planet.

The Event

A Festival at the Intersection of Technology & Humanity

Creative Code 4.0 brings together four distinct programmes — Community Service, IT Adventure, Technology Seminar, and Grand Celebration — spread across four days in November 2025.

Each programme is designed to engage a different dimension of the technology ecosystem: service, competition, learning, and community. Previous editions have attracted over 3,200 participants from 14 provinces nationwide.

THE ORGANIZER

About HIMATESI

HIMATESI (Himpunan Mahasiswa Teknologi Informasi) is the official Information Technology Student Association established in 2019. With over 500 active members, HIMATESI runs workshops, seminars, and competitions throughout the academic year.

Creative Code is HIMATESI's flagship annual festival — entering its fourth edition in 2025 — and is the largest student-organized technology event in the region.

Brief History
