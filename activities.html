01 -- COMMUNITY SERVICE

Baksos (Community Service)

Technology for Local Communities

Baksos is community service initiative that puts technology in the hands of those who need it most. Volunteers work with local communities to provide digital literacy training, tech support, and infrastructure setup -- embodying the Society 5.0 ethos of technology for humanity.

BENEFITS & OUTCOMES

  • Earn volunteer hours and community service certification
  • Gain hands-on experience with social technology projects
  • Network with fellow socially-conscious technologists
  • Receive official HIMATESI participation certificate

02 - COMPETITION

IT Adventure

Test Your Technical Limits

IT Adventure is a multi-track competitive event spanning web development, data analytics, UI/UX design, cybersecurity, and algorithmic problem solving. Teams of 2-3 members compete across a series of escalating challenges designed to test both depth and breadth of technical knowledge.

BENEFITS & OUTCOMES

  • Compete for cash prize totaling IDR 50,000,000
  • Win trophies, medals, and special awards
  • Get noticed by industry sponsors recruiters
  • Receive certificates of achievement for all finalists

03 - KNOWLEDGE

Technology Seminar

Insights from Industry Leaders

The Technology Seminar gathers leading voices from across the technology industry for a full day of keynotes, panel discussions, and workshops. Focused on Society 5.0, speakers explore AI ethics, smart cities, green technology, digital health, and the future of human-machine collaboration.

BENEFITS & OUTCOMES

  • Learn directly from CTO-level industry practitioners
  • Gain insights on emerging technologies and career pathways
  • Workshop sessions with hands-on learning components
  • Networking lunch and post-session meetups

04 - CELEBRATION

Grand Celebration

The Culminating Experience

The Grand Celebration is the spectacular closing ceremony of Creative Code 0.4 - an evening that honors the week's participants, announces competition winners, features live performances, and looks ahead to the future. it's an experience designed to inspire, connect, and commemorate.
